City Launches Open Data Hub
Date: 2025-09-01 Country: Canada
[Image Placeholder]

The City of Montreal has officially launched its open data hub, making building information more accessible to the public. The initiative is part of a global effort to promote transparency, innovation, and sustainable urban planning.

Researchers, policymakers, and citizens will be able to explore building data, collaborate on projects, and share insights that help cities grow more sustainably.

This project is supported by multiple institutions and contributes to the global Colouring Cities Research Programme.
